## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Why retry only 2 times?**
A: Balance between recovery and UX. 3+ retries = 5+ second delay that frustrates users.

**Q: Why return empty data instead of error?**
A: Better UX - shows "no results" than "something broke". Users understand empty state.

**Q: Why different timeouts (5s vs 15s)?**
A: Search is UI-blocking (show timeout faster). Reviews are backgroundloading (can wait longer).

**Q: Will this affect performance?**
A: Improves it - no more hanging requests. Worst case adds 500-2000ms delay on network errors.

**Q: What if backend is permanently down?**
A: App shows empty states. Users see "no inventory" vs "error". Better than crash.
